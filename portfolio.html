iMakeTime

iMakeTime is an iOS app that acts as a companion to a published self help book. I took this project on in 2019 and completed work for the client in about a year.

The idea behind iMakeTime is that happiness in life is achievable by finding success and fufillment in 3 main categories of life. This app makes this program easier to follow by allowing users to customize a plan for them, and track their progress throughout the day.

isbcpsclosed.com

Created when I was in highschool, isbcpsclosed.com crawled local news websites for school closing data in the winter, and would tell you whether or not our district was closed due to inclement weather.

To make the interface aestheticly pleasing, I learned about using CSS to animate things in a webpage, and added a basic snowfall effect to the background.

Eventose

Created at the University of Michigan's annual hackathon, Eventose is a product I created with a team that allows event attendees to check into conferences on their phone using bluetooth low-energy (BLE) beacons.

This project was created entirely within 36 hours, and was judged as an entry in the event competition. We were awarded the Most Professional prize by LinkedIn.

I worked on the front end for the web based admin portal, and assisted with the development of our android app.
